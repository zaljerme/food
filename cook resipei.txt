Ingredients:
. potato
. ground meat
. vegetable (oil)
. flour/ bread crums 





1. grab a potato and peel it
2. Cut it into thin pieces but not that thin 
3. then fill the ground meet inside the two thin peices of potato
4. then grab the potato that you have filled up with ground meet and dip the sides of the ground meet potato in the flour or bread crumms 
and roll it 360 degrees and have all the the sides dipped with the flour or bread crums
5. then fry the potatos untill cooked (you can make as much as you want)
